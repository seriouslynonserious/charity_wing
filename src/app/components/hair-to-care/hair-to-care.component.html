About Us

Uniting for a Noble Cause: Empowering Cancer Patients with Hair Donations

The Hair-to-Care wing of GMC Thrissur is dedicated to providing wigs and hairpieces for cancer patients undergoing chemotherapy. Our mission is to restore dignity, confidence, and hope to patients who have lost their hair due to treatment.

Join us in making a difference

Our team at Hair-to-Care works closely with volunteers and donors to collect hair donations, which are then used to create high-quality wigs for cancer patients. Every donation, no matter the size, helps us provide comfort and support to those going through one of life's toughest battles. Through this initiative, we aim to foster a sense of community and compassion, making a lasting impact in the lives of those in need.

How It Helps Cancer Patients

Cancer patients going through chemotherapy often face emotional challenges due to hair loss. Your donation provides them with wigs that restore their appearance, confidence, and dignity during their fight against cancer.

The Impact of Hair Loss

Hair loss due to chemotherapy is more than just physical; it’s an emotional challenge for cancer patients. By donating your hair, you help restore not only their appearance but also their mental and emotional well-being.
